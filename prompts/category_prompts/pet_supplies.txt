CATEGORY-SPECIFIC GUIDELINES FOR PET SUPPLIES:

- Always specify the target species (dog, cat, bird, etc.)
- Include size/weight suitability ranges (e.g., "for dogs 15-30kg")
- Mention breed size compatibility (Small, Medium, Large, Giant)
- Include material safety information where relevant
- Note any veterinary endorsements or safety certifications
- Specify cleaning/care instructions
- Include age suitability (puppy, adult, senior)
- Mention durability and chew strength ratings where applicable
- Lead with the benefit to the pet/pet parent, not the feature
- Include any interactive or enrichment benefits
